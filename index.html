About Me

Hi, my name is Sooky and I use the He/Him series pronouns.

Three words I would use to describe myself are creative, hands-on, and technical. I'm creative because I have a passion for creating and designing. I'm hands-on because I love working with my hands, whether tinkering with different types of devices or even cars. Finally, I'm technically-inclined because I enjoy working with, learning about, and using technology to make our lives and the world around us better.

I specialize in digital design, which includes logo creation, identity rebranding, presentations, social media content and copy creation, and more. I have experience creating websites with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, as well as through CMS platforms like WordPress. I have also done plenty of print design, including fliers, brochures, booklets, magazines, and certificates.

Aside from my graphic design work, I have experience with supervising, strategic planning, non-profit management, client relations, teaching, facilitating workshops and presentations, administering, data collection and management, and technical troubleshooting.

Lesley University

Cadillac Information Design

This project came from my love of cars and design. I chose the Cadillac brand because I have an appreciation for their styling and strong brand identity, plus I drive one.

The first piece consists of two timelines that tell the Cadillac story through time, and there are many different smaller details throughout.

The second piece is showcase of the different rear ends that cadillac has created throughout the years. It was inspired by Cadillac's jet-engine inspired tailfins from
the 50s and 60s.

My team created a new logo and color system, a fully functional website on WordPress, and various potential products for them to sell. My main contributions were setting up the WordPress site as well as concept product creation.
